Welcome to Tuneify

About Tuneify

Tuneify, a personal project created by a passionate student, is designed to help you delve deeper into your music preferences. By analyzing your listening history, Tuneify provides insights into your recent song obscurity, top artists, tracks, and genres. Additionally, it suggests recommended songs to enhance your music exploration.

How It Works

Tuneify analyzes your listening history from Spotify and presents detailed data to help you understand your musical inclinations better.
